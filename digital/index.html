kamysh #1 Art object

Concept sketch / hand drawing

A black industrial cube, 120 cm³, suspended from a chain. Inside, a school of approximately 20 glass fish, handcrafted using lampwork technique. Contour forms, transparent, resembling 3D wireframes. Each frozen in its own position.

Light cuts through the black void, refracting in the glass ribs. Delicate glass graphics against heavy metal: beautiful, technological, meaningless.

This is what remains after millennia. Not the internet, not neural networks, not debates. Wires, pipes, glass, metal, arranged in incomprehensible connections. Only to guess: who we worshipped, what we believed, why we did it all.

Object & Process

Specifications

The author reserves the right to vary the number of glass elements and specific design of each creature while maintaining the contrast between heavy industrial frame and fragile transparent content.

Materiality

Lampwork glass, black metal frame, steel chain. Cube 120 × 120 × 120 cm, suspended with lowest point approximately 150 cm from floor.

Lighting

Cold light source illuminates internal contents, causing refraction in glass structures.

Sculptural content

Approximately 20 unique glass fish, transparent borosilicate glass, skeletal wireframe construction. Each form unique, suspended on thin chains or monofilament.
